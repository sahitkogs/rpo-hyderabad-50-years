50 Years of Service 1976 · 2026

A Journey of 50 Years

From bifurcation in 1976 to a digital, citizen-first office today.

  1. 1976

    Office Established

    RPO Hyderabad is set up after bifurcation from the Chennai Passport Office, beginning its mandate of serving applicants across the region.

  2. 2015

    Going Public on Social Media

    The office joins X (formerly Twitter) as @rpohyderabad, opening a direct line of communication with citizens.

  3. 2017

    Expanding the PSK Network

    Passport Seva Kendras and Post Office PSKs across Telangana scale up to bring services closer to applicants.

  4. 2020

    Service Continuity Through the Pandemic

    Through lockdowns and restrictions, the office adapts processes to keep emergency and essential passport services running.

  5. 2023

    New Leadership

    Jonnalagadda Snehaja, IFS assumes charge as Regional Passport Officer on 24 November 2023 — the second woman to lead RPO Hyderabad.

  6. 2024

    Wait Times Slashed

    Appointment wait time falls from 22 days to 6–8 days, with further improvement to 1–10 days for regular applicants.

  7. 2025

    e-Passports & National Recognition

    e-Chip passports launched in March. In June, RPO Hyderabad wins the national award for innovative, citizen-centric initiatives at the Regional Passport Officers' Conference.

  8. 2026

    50 Years of Service

    A milestone year — celebrating five decades of trust, reform, and service to citizens of Telangana.

  • Service Indian Foreign Service, 2016 batch
  • Charge assumed 24 November 2023
  • Background Chartered Accountant; AIR 103, UPSC CSE 2015
  • Hometown Hyderabad
  • Earlier postings Embassy of India, Beijing; MEA Divisions, New Delhi

Innovations & Reforms · 2024–2025

Citizen-first changes, made everyday.

  • Thursday Walk-in Grievance Redressal

    Every Thursday, 9:30 AM – 12:30 PM, citizens can walk in without an appointment to have their grievance heard. Tens of thousands have been served.

  • e-Chip Passports & Passport Seva 2.0

    Embedded chip passports for stronger security and faster immigration; the online portal upgraded with clearer flows and integrated chatbots.

  • Mobile Passport Van

    A dedicated van takes passport services on the road for outreach drives, special groups, and accessibility-focused camps.

  • Cyber Awareness Campaigns

    Regular drives to help citizens spot scams, protect their documents, and use the official portal safely.

  • Swachhata & Rainwater Harvesting

    A revitalised office premises and on-site rainwater harvesting — small habits, sustained over time.

  • Constitution @75 Envelope

    A commemorative passport envelope released to mark 75 years of the Constitution of India.

  • Art & Calligraphy Exhibition

    A year-long exhibition on the Constitution, inaugurated by the Governor of Telangana.

  • Personal Email Review

    The RPO personally reviews ~100 emails received daily; over 10,000 complaints resolved through email.

Services at a Glance

Everything you need, in one place.

All applications are submitted through the official portal — passportindia.gov.in. This page is a quick reference, not the application portal.

Passport Types

  • Fresh / Re-issue
  • Tatkal (urgent)
  • Minor's passport
  • Police Clearance Certificate
  • Diplomatic / Official (eligibility-based)

Typical Timelines

  • Regular: 5–7 working days (post verification)
  • Tatkal: 1–3 working days
  • Speed Post delivery: ~24 hours after dispatch
  • Appointment wait: 1–10 days regular; 1–5 days Tatkal

Documents Checklist

  • Proof of present address
  • Proof of date of birth
  • Photographs (per official spec)
  • Old passport (for re-issue)
  • Parental documents (for minors)

Full checklists on the official portal.
